THE ENIGMATIC CONUNDRUM

Welcome to Room 4 on the Escape room. Congratulations on making it this far. In this room you will be asked to answer some riddles.

RULES

  1. Access to the subsequent riddle will not be permitted until the previous riddle is solved.
  2. After a certain amount of time passes without a riddle being solved, you will have the choice of using a hint.
  3. Once you begin, all riddles must be solved within 10 minutes.
  4. If all riddles aren't solved, the self-destuct mechanism will trigger causing GAME OVER for the player.
